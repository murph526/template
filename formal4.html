Communication Section

Communication is another extreme part of SLP as well, as it is with many other majors. SLP’s have to communicate with patients on a daily basis. It’s the core aspect of the field. Students going into this field need to know that communication is the single most important aspect of this discipline. The most common type of communication in this field is the communication between the SLP and l patients. When studying in this field it is a good idea to familiarize yourself with how to communicate with others, especially from a medical standpoint. Being able to communicate with patients is the most important part of SLP.
