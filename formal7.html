Conclusion

College can be very stressful for students, especially incoming freshman. It is important that students know what they are getting themselves into when they study their major of choice at a university. In order to be successful, students need to know what is expected of them regarding the amount of work, and difficulty of work they are required to do. This report shows students what is expected of them academically, and what to expect when starting a career in the field of SLP.
