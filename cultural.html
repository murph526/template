Executive Summary

For my Cultural Anaylsis project I will be writing about how the social issue of poverty is shown through the TV show Shameless. Shameless is a great example of a form of technology that shows the social issue of poverty in our world today. Shameless depicts the south side of Chicago as a place of great poverty and shows viewers how being in poverty affects their lives.
