Blog Entry 1

The purpose of this video was for Uncle Ronnie's nephew to give him the message that his death had affected his live in a huge way and that they were alike in many different ways. He wanted to let his Uncle know that he too was going through similar problems that he was while he was letting his family and friends know that he was gay. He wanted to let his Uncle know that even though he had hard times while he was going through this stage in his life, that it would have gotten better like it did for him. His Uncle taking his own life hurt him because he was going through the same things his Uncle was. His purpose of the video was to tell his Uncle that he didn't have to take his life and that things would have gotten better, like they did for him. Not only is this message for his Uncle, but it is also for other people in the world that are going through these same problems. It is to let them know that things do get better. The video achieves its purpose by showing the hurt that Uncle Ronnie's nephew had by even telling the story. His nephew telling his story and letting people see the things he had to go through shows people going through the same problems that things will get better and that it is not taking your life over it. It really achieves it's purpose by showing the hurt that his nephew went through and showing that eventually things got better for him. This message had an extremely strong messaage and was structured through the various ways he told the story. In the beginning of the video he showed his letter he had written. He then shared a poem that really stood out to him and felt that it reflected parts of his own life. Actually seeing him tell the story and seeing his pain when telling the story really gets the message across to the viewers. This videos structure will help me revise my Lived Experience project because I now have seen a story told with a sucessful structure that allows viewers to stay engaged in the message that is trying to be shared.

Blog Entry 3

The Chrysler commercial for the Chrysler 200 portrays Detroit in a different way than most think of the city as. Detroit has been to hell and back and has had some very big down falls. The commercial doesn’t try to sugar coat what Detroit has been through, instead the creators chose to think of Detroit’s past as a building block that has made it a much stronger city. Because of this, the Chrysler 200 is seen to be a strong car. The song lose yourself by Eminem is included in this video due to the fact that he was born and raised in Detroit. Eminem is included to show that even though Detroit may be a not so great city currently, and even though it has had some hard falls, good can still come out of the city. Eminem is a very successful rap artist that is from Detroit. He has shown that Detroit is not a city full of failure. This commercial gives viewers a different perspective of Detroit that it is a very strong and unique city, just like the Chrysler 200. The tag line “Imported from Detroit” sends a very strong message to viewers. The definition of the word import from Merriam-Webster is to bring a product into a country to be sold. The main word that sticks out of that definition is country. Obviously, Detroit is in the United States, not in a different country, so why include the word imported? This is done to show how unique Detroit is. The US does not have any other city like Detroit. Detroit stands out, it is anything but ordinary. Detroit is like another country compared to the rest of the US. The creators included this to show how extraordinary Detroit is, along with the Chrysler 200. The main purpose of this ad is to show viewers how great of a car the Chrysler 200 is. By doing this, they compare it to Detroit, where the car is from, showing how strong and unique the car is, just like Detroit.
